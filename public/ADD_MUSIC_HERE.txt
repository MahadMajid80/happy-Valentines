To add background music to your Valentine's website:

1. Get a romantic song (MP3 format recommended)
2. Rename it to: romantic-music.mp3
3. Place it in this folder (/public/)
4. The music toggle button will automatically work!

For detailed instructions, see: HOW_TO_ADD_MUSIC.md

Supported formats: MP3, WAV, OGG, M4A
Recommended file size: Under 5MB

Free romantic music sources:
- Pixabay Music: https://pixabay.com/music/search/romantic/
- Free Music Archive: https://freemusicarchive.org/
